MELBOURNE

TRANSPORTATION GUIDE

This is page is dedicated towards guiding the visitors towards the process of transportation. It includes information on the listed locations on the website which includes the Rialto Towers, Melbourne Zoo, Luna Park, Crown and the Melbourne Cricket Grown.

It gives you an idea on which routes to go on both trams and trains and also gives you the distance which you need to travel from La Trobe University. For online bookings, you can email us by pressing the button below.

TRANSPORTATION ROUTES

Rialto Towers

Distance: ~12 km

Tram: Take Tram 86 or Tram 48 from La Trobe University to the Docklands (Collins Street) and transfer to Tram 70 or Tram 75, which stops at Rialto Towers.

Train: Take the train from Bundoora RMIT station to Southern Cross Station. From there, it's a short walk (approximately 10 minutes) to Rialto Towers.

Melbourne Zoo

Distance: ~8 km

Tram: Take Tram 86 from La Trobe University to Glenferrie Station. Transfer to Tram 55, which goes directly to Melbourne Zoo.

Train: Take the train from Bundoora RMIT station to North Melbourne Station, then catch Bus 505 to Melbourne Zoo.

Luna Park

Distance: ~14 km

Tram: Take Tram 86 from La Trobe University towards Waterfront City. Transfer to Tram 16 at Fitzroy Street to reach Luna Park in St Kilda.

Train: Take the train from Bundoora RMIT station to Flinders Street Station. From there, take Tram 96 or 112 from Bourke Street to St Kilda.

Crown Hotel

Distance: ~11 km

Take Tram 86 or Tram 48 from La Trobe University to Docklands (Collins Street). Transfer to Tram 12 or Tram 96, which will take you directly to Crown.

Train: Take the train from Bundoora RMIT station to Southern Cross Station. Crown Hotel is a short walk from the station.

Melbourne Cricket Ground

Distance: ~10 km

Take Tram 86 from La Trobe University towards Waterfront City. Transfer to Tram 75 at Collins Street and get off at Rod Laver Arena, which is a short walk to MCG.
