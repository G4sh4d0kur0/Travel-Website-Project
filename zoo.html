MELBOURNE ZOO

Learn about over 320 species around the world by visiting the Melbourne zoo. The zoo features a design which incorporates Asian and African rainforests, emulating the natural habitats of its diverse inhabitants. The zoo values conservation and education above all and offers it to all visitors of the Melbourne Zoo.

ANIMALS

The zoo offers a wide variety of species from the strong and big gorillas and elephants to species native from Australian wildlife which includes koalas, wombats, and the iconic kangaroos. The zoo features exhibits like the Orangutan Sanctuary and Butterfly House, providing an opportunity to watch the creatures up close. There is also The Trial of elephants, which offers a glimpse into these giant creatures way of life in a tropical setting. Additionally the Austrialian Bush section showcases iconic species such as emus, platypuses and Tasmanian devils.
