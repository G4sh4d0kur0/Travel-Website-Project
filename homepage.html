The Melbourne Cricket Ground

WATCH LEGENDS PLAY!

It is the place to be right now if you're a sports fan. It’s buzzing with excitement and action, from international cricket matches and intense AFL games to thrilling soccer and rugby events. Every game at this iconic stadium is an experience you won’t forget. Whether you’re cheering on cricket legends, soaking up the AFL Grand Final atmosphere, or catching a top soccer match, the MCG is where the action is. Grab your tickets and dive into the live sports scene together with other lively sport fans at one of the world’s most famous venues! Join the action at MCG!
