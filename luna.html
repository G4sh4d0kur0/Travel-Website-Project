RIDES

Enjoy the rides, Luna Park has to offer which includes Pharoah's Curse, challenging you to be flung 90 meters into the air, The Great Scenic Railway, which is the right world's oldest continually operating rollercoaster, Super Nova which would let you see a 360-degree view of the sky, Twin Dragon, taking you to swing on a giant dragon ship, Speedy Beetle, a rotating roller coaster. Take a relaxing view, venturing to a galactic journey as gravity shifts and your world begin to spin.
